## Beat sheet (target 1:55–2:05)

### 0:00–0:15 — Hook (personal, named)

> "Hey Bahaar — quick follow-up to our call. I wanted to show you something we already had running before we met. This is LIRE-Help, our AI ops layer for light industrial. Berkeley Partners is the anchor tenant modeled inside it — not because we're presumptuous, but because your portfolio is exactly what this was built for."

### 0:15–0:45 — The demo property is yours

*Screen: dashboard, Berkeley Partners tenant → Oakland Gateway property.*

> "Oakland Gateway — 185,000 sq ft, 12 units, 18 loading docks. This is a stand-in for one of your California sites. You'd see your real properties here, pulled from Yardi."

*Hover the occupancy + permit cards.*

> "Occupancy, active permits, expiring COIs, vendor contracts — all surfaced at the asset level. The Fire Occupancy Permit is flagged because it's in renewal. That's the compliance-blindspot problem you mentioned."

### 0:45–1:15 — The AI concierge, answering tenant questions

*Screen: chat widget on the Oakland Gateway landing page.*

> "Now the tenant side. A real tenant asks a maintenance question…"

*Type: "Loading dock 3 door is jammed, can't close it. Unit 4."*

> "…and the agent logs the ticket, cites the right vendor SLA, and offers an interim workaround. Human staff see this in the dashboard and only get pinged when something actually needs judgment. 90% autonomous, 10% escalation."

### 1:15–1:40 — Tie it to the two pilots

*Screen: the proposal doc or a simple split-slide.*

> "On our call we scoped two pilots. Pilot A is a lightweight leasing workspace — the 20% of VTS your brokers actually use plus the 30% they *should* be using, on top of Yardi. Pilot B is tenant financial review automation — your existing checklists, run by a Claude agent with a human approver and an SEC-archivable log."

### 1:40–1:55 — Why Dehyl, in one sentence

> "You already have the Claude and ChatGPT enterprise licenses, you already have the checklists, and you already have Azure. You don't need another platform or another strategy deck. You need a small team that has shipped this exact pattern in your exact vertical — and can co-design the compliance piece with you instead of around you. That's us."

### 1:55–2:05 — Ask

> "Send the VTS link and the financial review process doc whenever you're ready — I'll turn around a tighter Pilot A scope within five business days. Thanks Bahaar."
