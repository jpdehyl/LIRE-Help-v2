THE PLATFORM

One platform. Every tenant touchpoint covered.

Not a chatbot bolted onto your website — an operations layer that integrates with how tenants actually communicate.

Core Feature
🤖

AI Tenant Concierge

Answers tenant questions 24/7 — loading dock schedules, parking rules, lease terms, building access. Knows each property's specific details. Handles maintenance request intake with structured data collection (unit, description, urgency). Escalates to the right person when human judgment is needed.

🔧

Maintenance Operations

Structured work order creation with automatic routing. Tenants submit via chat, receive ticket numbers and ETAs. PM dashboard shows all open tickets, SLA status, and resolution history.

📊

Property Dashboard

Real-time visibility across your portfolio. Active conversations, open maintenance tickets, tenant satisfaction scores, response time metrics — one screen, all properties.

📚

Property Knowledge Base

Each property gets its own knowledge base — dock hours, emergency procedures, vendor contacts, lease specifics. The concierge learns from it. You edit it in plain English.

🔔

Smart Escalation

Not everything needs AI. Emergencies, billing disputes, and lease negotiations route to the right person via email, SMS, or Slack — with full conversation context attached.

📈

Tenant Analytics

Understand your tenants better. Conversation volume, common issues, satisfaction trends, and early churn signals — all derived from actual interactions, not annual surveys.
